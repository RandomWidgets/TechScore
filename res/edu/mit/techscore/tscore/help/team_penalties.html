Team penalties

Enter team penalties for one team in a given division.

A team penalty is a penalty of +20 points assigned to a specific team in a given division. In college sailing, these penalties are award for missing RP or illegal pinnies, for example. This pane is similar to the pane for entering individual penalties.

New penalty

To enter a new penalty, choose the team and the division from the lists provided. Next, choose the penalty type, four of which are provided:

  • PFD: Illegal Personal Floation Device
  • LOP: Missing identification
  • MRP: Missing Record of Participation (RP)
  • GDQ: General disqualification (misc.)

As mentioned, all team penalties are scored as +20 points per division after all races have been scored.

Optionally enter any comments for the penalty and click on the Add button to add the penalty.

Current penalties

This section lists all the team penalties currently in the regatta. To drop an existing team penalty, click on the button labeled Drop next to that penalty. The regatta will be rescored accordingly.
