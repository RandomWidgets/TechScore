Finishes

Enter or edit finishes for a specific race

Parameters

First choose the race for which you want to enter a finish. In case of combined division scoring, choose the race in any division. You can also use the spinner to cycle through the current races.

If rotations have been setup, you can choose to enter finishes using sail numbers instead of team names. Choose the appropriate option from the Using drop down menu.

Teams & Order

The main part of the pane consists of two lists, called Teams and Order. Move the appropriate team or sail from the first list to another by clicking on each team at a time. If entering finishes using rotations, you can also enter the sail numbers directly in the textbox above the two lists and press Enter on your keyboard to move that sail over.

Once on the second list, you can reshuffle the teams by clicking and dragging an item on the list. Clicking on the item in the second list will push it back to the first list.

When all the teams have been moved to the second list, you will be able to click on the button in the bottom right labeled Enter finish to score that race. Only then will that race be officially added to the regatta. If you attempt to change races or leave the pane with partially entered finishes, TechScore will prompt you to enter or abandon those finishes.

To remove finishes for an already scored race, click on the Remove finish button in the bottom left.

To view the scores, go to View>View scores.
