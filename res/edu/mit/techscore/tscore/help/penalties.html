Penalties

Enter individual penalties for one team in a given race.

New penalty

Enter penalties similarly to the way finishes are entered. Choose the race from the list and the type of penalty. Only five penalties types are provided consistent with college sailing rules. All penalties are scored Fleet + 1.

If rotations are being used, you can opt to enter penalties by choosing the sail number instead of the team name.

Choose the team from the drop down list (one at a time). The teams you can choose depend on which teams already have penalties in that race (no team can have more than one penalty).

Optionally enter a brief commentary on the reasons for the penalty in the box labeled Comment. Click on the button Enter penalty to finish.

Current penalties

Under this section you will see the current penalties entered in the regatta. You may drop a penalty by clicking on the Drop button. The team will be reinstated to their original finish before the penalty and the regatta will be rescored.
